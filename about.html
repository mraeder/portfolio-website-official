Bio

I had never even seen a line of code until 2020. After dreaming up a cool travel app idea, my research on what it takes to actually build such an app led to intrigue, which led to exploring software technologies and careers. I was consumed by the seemingly endless possibilities in the industry. I wrote my first lines of code with some online courses on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I then pursued a JavaScript coding bootcamp and soon got accepted into Genesis10's highly competitive Dev10 program, which prepared me with a Java bootcamp before I joined Northwestern Mutual. If you’re interested in a career pivot into tech or are entering tech via a non-traditional path, reach out to me via email or LinkedIn (I am very friendly and software is pretty neat!).
