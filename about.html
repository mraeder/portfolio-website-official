Bio

I had never considered a career in tech until earlier this year, when I started dreaming up an idea for a travel app. After researching what it takes to actually produce such an app, my intrigue led me to explore tech careers. I became consumed with the seemingly endless possibilities in the tech industry. I wrote my first lines of code with some Udemy courses on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python and Harvard's CS50x Intro to Computer Science course, and then enrolled in a 5+ month software development bootcamp. I am eager to enter such a rich, challenging, and growing field. At the end of the day, I just want to build cool, complex stuff that makes a meaningful difference in our world!
