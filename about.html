About

Bio

Though I have a background in business psychology, sales, and project management, I was bitten by the tech bug in early 2020 and have been programming nearly every day since. After trying out a few different early career paths, tech is alluring to me because it offers an opportunity to live my values: the pursuit of ambitious ideas, lifelong learning, building relationships with genuine people, and making meaningful contributions to the future of our world. I started off teaching myself to code with online resources and loved it so much that I am currently enrolled in a 5+ month software development bootcamp to continue building my skills.
