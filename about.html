I had never considered a career in tech until earlier this year, when I started dreaming up an idea for a travel buddy app. After researching what it takes to actually produce such an app, my intrigue led me to explore tech careers. A couple previous career paths quickly left me bored and hungry for a meaningful challenge, so I became enamored with the depth, complexity, and immensity of the tech industry. I wrote my first lines of code with some Udemy courses on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python and Harvard's CS50x Intro to Computer Science course, and then enrolled in a 5+ month software development bootcamp. I am super excited to enter such a broad field that seems to be riddled with endless learning and opportunities to make a meaningful difference in our world!
