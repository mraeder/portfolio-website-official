Money Trees



Summary


In my coding bootcamp, we had the opportunity to take a project concept and develop it using each of the technologies we learned throughout the bootcamp. I built Money Trees as a fictitious crowdfunding website for entrepreneurs and creators where users can learn more about Money Trees as a crowdfunding host, explore active campaigns plus their goals and progress, and inquire about starting their own campaign. This project helped me take my new React skills for a test drive!
